Funds Metrics

  • Alpha: The excess return of the investment relative to the return of the benchmark index. The large the better
  • Beta: A measure of a particular fund's movement (ups and downs) compared to its benchmark index. Systmatic Risk
  • Sharp Ratio: Measures risk-adjusted performance. Rate of return dividing it's standard deviation. The large the better
  • Uncertainty is on both side, upside uncertainty is good for investor.
  • It is risk adjusted return that matters.

Random Walks - Fair Game

The Setup

  • A gambler with initial $1000 in her pocket.
  • Bet 1$ each time, she will win or lose $1 on each bet with probability 1/2.
  • She will play until she is broke or she has won 100$, and back to home happly with $1100.

The Question

  • If she come casino with $1,000, What's the probability she go home with $1,100.
  • If she come casino with $1,000,000, What's the probability she go home with $1,000,100.

The Answer

  • 1000/1100=10/11
  • 100000/100100=10,000/10,001

OHLC - Model

  • Open: The most important price
  • Chart Representation: Open High Low Close
  • Maximum Adverse Excursion, MAE (Loss)
  • Maximum Favorable Excursion, MFE (Profit)

Pseudo Code 

1 puts "hello word"
2 set  openToHigh [expr $High - $Open]; 
3 set  openToLow  [expr $Open - $Low ];
4 set  MAE        MathMin($openToHigh $openToLow);
5 set  MFE        MathMax($openToHigh $openToLow);
6 set  midMAF     MathMidian(MAE  n-20  n)  
7 set  midMFF     MathMidian(MFE  n-20  n)
