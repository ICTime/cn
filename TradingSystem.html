Position Limit for each Daily System

  • We trade about 10 markets
    • Soybean: RM M Y A
    • Indstrial RB RU
    • Chemical PTA L PP
    • Agreculture JD BB SR
  • The limits were:
Levle Type Maximum Unites
1 Single Market 2 Unites
2 Closely Correlated Markets 6 Units
3 Loosely Correlated Markets 10 Units
4 Single Direction – Long or Short 15 Unite

Note:

  • one unit is 1% of initial Account Balance
  • Industrial and Chemical are closely correlated
  • Soybean and Agreculture is loosly correlated.
  • Locked in profit positions are not subject to this rule.

Rolling Contract

  • 01 05 09/10 are the dominant contract for most of the markets.
  • When meet position limit, open new position in the the next dominant contract
  • Checking trading signal on two contract with largest open intrest.
  • If Position is closed by contract expiration, Use DIBS to reopen new ones in the next contract with full position limit.

Turtle System

This system is used to capture the large trend, This could sever as Holding overnight position for the 5Min Day Trading system.

Entry
1 unit long at 20 Day High, 1 unit short at 20 Day Low.
Default entry 50 Day High/Low if trade is missed by skip rule
Exit
10 Day Low for long, 10 Day High for short
Skip
Skip trade if the last trade is successful

Single Moving Average

This system is used to capture the large trend, This could sever as
Holding overnight position for the 5Min Day Trading system.

Trend
Whe a Key Bar is on 30 Day MA, we trade with the direction of slop MA30
10 Day High/Low if trade is missed by slop or skip rule.
Entry
Key Bar High/Low Breakout with 3 Day Low/High stop for 1 unit
Skip
Stop trade after two consective loss on the MA
Exit
10 Day High/Low or 30MA which comes first for profit trade

Daily Break Out

This mainly serve as holding position mechanism for holding overnight position for [Day Trading]{#MBBO}. Positions exceeded the limit (Risked) is droped off at the close of the day.

Setup
Trade Next day if today’s Day Bar is IB/NR4/NR7/GANN Break point/ Balance of CLose

Note:

  • NR4 is Narrest Range Bar in 4 days and today is Inside Bar
  • GANN Bar is two consective lower high and Lower low in uptrend, and high high and high low in down trend.
  • Balance of Close is smallest 3 close range in 10 days.
Entry
Key Bar High/Low BreakOut with stop on Low/High of KeyBar for 1 unit
If Gap outside of KeyBar, Trade in direction of MA30 slop with MAE5 as stop loss
Exit
3 Bar High/Low Trailing stop for the profit position

Minutes Bar Break Out

This is an intra-day trend trading system frame entry. The main purpose of this system is accumulation of large position in the intra-day swing.

If there is trade signle on Daily System, Holding overnight postion according to the risk rules, Otherwise exit all at the close

Base Line

Entry
The setup is INR4/NR7/Doji 5 Minutes Bar. Stop order on H/L, No Bias, Take each IB/doji on the BO direction
Exit
TP half position at 1:1RR, Hold the risk free trade till Close.
Misc
Max take two trades if the next bars is outside bar, otherwise trade only in the direction of first Breakout.
Variation

The follwing tactics can for controling slippage and imporve expactency.

  • if IB HL is 1 or 2 ticks within the TD High/Low on the opposite short term trend, use TD for stoploss.
  • On the doji bars, Open opsition on the next bar open with limit order on the close.
  • 2X if the BO agree with the 5Min SR trend
  • Adding to lose postion before hit 1:1 Stop loss, this is the tactics to open large amount of postion.

Position Sizing

Note:

Trading multiple size when all we favore one ore more of the follwing direction. For example, if 30 Dailay MA is up, we trade 2X size in the long side of Base Line strategy.

  • Long Term Trend: which is defined by 30MA slop.
  • Short Term Trend with the Day swing leg, up trend is good as long as close is above 3 bar low.
  • Relative High/Low of Day Bar SR line, Follow the Breakout or Fade it.
  • Balance point: 3Bar Close, Open Price, Gann Bar Break point, lastH in Daily Donw trend.
  • No Adding on opposite direction after exceed lastH/lastL.

TD Breakout

  • After the NBBO Fail, Change to the TD point Break out mode
  • Take only the setup where TD High Low range is smaller than the Median, or 25% quantile
  • Max two trade in 3 bars, and stop trading on failing 2 directions
